Instructions on How to Play Crash


Here is how to play crash, the game is simple to play...


1. Select the difficulty of the game


2. Place your bet in the box and hit start


3. Click the cashout button before the rocket crashes


If you run out of money and your balance is 0, simply hit the reset balance button


Enjoy the game, and good luck!
